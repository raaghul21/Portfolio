Project Two

A gas leakage detector is a safety device designed to detect the presence of gas leaks and alert users to potential hazards. It uses sensors to monitor the concentration of specific gases, such as natural gas, propane, or methane. When a leak is detected, the device triggers an alarm, which may be audible, visual, or both. Detectors can be portable or fixed, with some featuring digital displays for real-time gas level monitoring. They are crucial in residential, commercial, and industrial settings to prevent accidents, fires, and health risks associated with gas leaks.
